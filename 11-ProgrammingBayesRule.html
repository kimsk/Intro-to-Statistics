  • P(H|C1) = 0.5
  • P(H|C2) = 0.9

What is P(H)?

  • P(H) = P(C1,H)+P(C2,H)
  • P(C1,H) = P(C1)*P(H|C1)
  • P(C2,H) = P(C2)*P(H|C2)
1: 
2: 
3: 
4: 
5: 

let ``P(C1)`` = 0.3
let ``P(H|C1)`` = 0.5
let ``P(H|C2)`` = 0.9

``P(C1)``*``P(H|C1)`` + (1.-``P(C1)``)*``P(H|C2)``
 
0.78
